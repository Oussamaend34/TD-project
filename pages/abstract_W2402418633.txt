What is known about this topic Chronic pain often has a psychic impact; Chronic neck pain, as an example, was been associated with psychosocial distress; Chronic low back pain can be a cause of anxiety and/or depression. But no study assessed the prevalence of anxiety and depressive disorders in chronic neck pain patients.
